    If you find yourself interacting with an immigration officer in any location including your workplace, or out in the community, remember that:

    YOU SHOULD:

    • Remain calm and keep your hands where the officer can see them

    YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO:

    • Remain slent when questions or arrested by immigration officers
    • NOT discuss your immigration or citizenship status with the police, immigration agents, or other officials
    • NOT sign anything you do not understand. Instead, state that you wish to speak with an attorney
    • Click here to find an immigration attorney and other legal resources

    If an officer knocks on your door:

    • Do not open of the door.
    • Teach your children not to open the door
    • Officers must have a warrant signed by a judge to enter your home
