    • Prepare for Safety

      Always be attentive to your own vulnerability and act accordingly
      Install the Signal app on your phone and use it for any sensitive activity
      Turn off Mobile Advertising ID on your phone
      Turn off Face ID on your phone when engaging in risky behavior
      Wear a whistle
      Store in your phone or memorize the number of ICIRR (or NLG if you are a citizen) - see below
      Make sure you let your loved ones and housemates know when you are engaging in risky activity and when you expect to be home
      Make sure you have a plan for childcare or pickup before engaging in risky activity
      Consider wearing a facemask, both to stop spread of respiratory diseases and to prevent surveillance
      Do not borrow vehicles of vulnerable people when engaging in risky activity
      DO NOT SHARE ANY INFORMATION with LAW ENFORCEMENT, including FBI, ICE, DEA, CBP, CPD, ATF, or CCSO.
